Wave Beta Privacy Notice

Last updated: 22 July 2026

Wave is currently a product prototype. Workspace information is stored in your browser using local storage. Wave does not currently create a cloud account, synchronize workspace data between people, or automatically receive the information you enter.

Information you choose to enter

Do not enter passwords, payment credentials, private keys, confidential client information, personal financial information, or regulated personal data. Use fictional or non-sensitive test information.

Feedback

The in-product feedback tool creates a file on your device. It is not transmitted automatically. If you share that file through GitHub, email, or another service, that service's privacy terms apply.

Local storage and deletion

Your browser retains demo workspace data on that device. You can remove it using Reset demo workspace in Settings or by clearing site data in your browser.

Hosting

The beta is hosted by Vercel, which may process technical request information under its own policies.

Contact

For beta questions or deletion requests concerning information you deliberately sent to the Wave team, open an issue at github.com/Andallyn/Wave/issues.

This notice is an early-stage operational draft and should receive legal review before commercial launch.
