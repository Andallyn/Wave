Wave Beta Terms

Last updated: 22 July 2026

Wave is provided as an experimental beta for evaluation and feedback. It is not a production service and may change, lose locally stored data, or be temporarily unavailable.

No sensitive or live operational data

Use sample or non-sensitive information only. Do not rely on Wave to publish content, contact partners, execute payments, preserve records, or make business decisions.

No warranties

The beta is provided “as is” without warranties of availability, accuracy, fitness for a particular purpose, or data retention.

Acceptable testing

Do not attempt unauthorized access, disrupt the service, upload unlawful content, impersonate another person, or use the beta in a way that harms others.

Feedback

You may voluntarily provide feedback to help improve Wave. Do not include third-party confidential information in feedback reports.

Ending participation

You may stop using the beta at any time and clear its browser data. Access may also be limited or withdrawn while the product is developed.

Contact

Questions can be raised through the Wave GitHub repository.

These beta terms are an early-stage operational draft and should receive legal review before commercial launch.
